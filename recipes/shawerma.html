Shawerma~~

Discription..

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish made of thinly sliced meat (typically lamb, chicken, or beef) cooked on a vertical rotisserie. The meat is usually served wrapped in a flatbread or pita along with vegetables and sauces like tahini or garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced meat (lamb, chicken, or beef)
  • Flatbread or pita
  • Vegetables
  • Sauces (tahini, garlic sauce)

Steps..

  1. Season and marinate the meat.
  2. Stack the marinated meat slices on a vertical rotisserie.
  3. Cook the meat slowly as it rotates on the rotisserie.
  4. Slice the cooked meat thinly.
  5. Serve the sliced meat in a flatbread or pita with vegetables and sauces.
