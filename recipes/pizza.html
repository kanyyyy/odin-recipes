Pizza~~

Discription..

Pizza is a classic Italian dish consisting of a round, flat dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps..

  1. Roll out dough.
  2. Spread sauce.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake.
