Fried chicken~~

Discription..

Fried chicken is a dish made by coating chicken pieces in seasoned flour or batter and frying them until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Flour
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, etc.)
  • Oil for frying

Steps..

  1. Coat chicken pieces in seasoned flour.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan.
  3. Fry chicken until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
